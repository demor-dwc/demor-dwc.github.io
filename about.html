William Kennamore

Master Trainer, Wellness & Lifestyle Coach, DEMOR HotSpot Therapist

William is a published author (Your Personal Trainer, The Comprehensive Guide to Fitness and Health, Combining Physical & Mental Exercises with Current Medical Facts, 1998). He currently works as a Master Trainer and Bodywork Specialist.

William has served as a Fitness Director, operated a wellness program and managed my own personal training studio. He has also served as a commission officer in the United States Marine Corps. Clients benefit from his leadership experiences and no-excuse attitude.

Will has worked with professional athletes, housewives, and everyone in between.
