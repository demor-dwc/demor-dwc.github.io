What is DEMOR?

Determined Energy Motivates Ongoing Results

DEMOR is a psycho-physical/emotional philosophy that emphasizes a system approach to life. DEMOR (Deliver Enhanced Movement Optimal Results incorporates) HotSpot Therapy (a new Massage Technique) and T.E.A.M. W.O.R.K.O.U.T. (a new concept in Personal Fitness) along with a proper balance of nutrition: carbohydrates, proteins and fats for a holistic approach to a fit way of living.

The DEMOR System approach to life is designed to be used as instructions for overall mental, physical and emotional soundness. Most importantly it is structured to be a blueprint for proper thinking and physically looking and feeling good about you.

DEMOR System begins with a daily workout with a proper balance of nutrition: carbohydrates, proteins and fats; followed by a good dose of feeling good about you to help maintain a determined mindset so you are able to move beyond “I should, could, would or ought to” thinking.
